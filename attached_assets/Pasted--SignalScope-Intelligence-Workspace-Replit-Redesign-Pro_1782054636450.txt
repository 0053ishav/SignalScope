# SignalScope Intelligence Workspace — Replit Redesign Prompt

Build a modern SaaS Intelligence Workspace for SignalScope.

This is NOT a music player.

This is NOT a lyrics viewer.

This is NOT a track details page.

SignalScope is an AI-powered Music Intelligence Platform that transforms lyrics, themes, moods, metadata, RichSync timelines, and Musixmatch analysis into actionable audience intelligence for artists, labels, marketers, managers, and A&R teams.

The design should feel like a blend of:

* Google AI Studio
* OpenAI Platform
* Palantir Foundry
* Amplitude
* Mixpanel
* Datadog
* Stripe Dashboard
* Linear
* Vercel Dashboard

The experience should feel premium, enterprise-grade, data-driven, and intelligence-focused.

Avoid generic music app layouts.

Avoid Spotify-style interfaces.

Avoid large album-art-first layouts.

Prioritize intelligence and decision-making.

---
